Our Story

Craft coffee
in a tranquil space

Paper Wings opened on Silver Street with a single purpose — to make specialty coffee feel accessible, unhurried, and genuinely delicious. We source exceptional beans, brew them with care using methods that honour the craft, and pair them with homemade bakes that change with the seasons.

The name says it all: light, delicate, and always on its way somewhere beautiful.

What We Serve

Simple things,
done perfectly

Our menu is intentionally focused — every item has earned its place. Nothing is here by accident.

Brewed to order

Pour Over

Single origin beans brewed through our Origami dripper. Jasmine, guava, strawberry — the cup tells the story of where it was grown.

Espresso bar

Flat White & Lattes

Silky oat or whole milk, pulled over a precise espresso. The decaf oat latte tastes like the real thing — because it is.

House specialty

One & One

A shot of espresso, a mini flat white, and still water on a hand-crafted tray. The full ritual in a single order.

Homemade daily

Japanese Swirl Cake

Light, yielding, impossibly delicate. Our swirl cake has become something of a signature — best enjoyed with a pour over alongside.

Baked in-house

Banana Bread & Bakes

Banana bread that's moist where it should be, golden where it needs to be. Plus seasonal pastries from our kitchen.

Fresh rolls

Cream Roll

Fresh cream, strawberries, soft sponge. A quiet luxury. The kind of thing you come back for the next day, and the day after that.
